About

Pegasus is a Browser extension that act as a bridge between your browser and the IOTA Tangle. It allows you to run IOTA Dapps in your browser without running a node. Moreover, Pegasus allows developers to build IOTA Dapps without paying attention on how to keep the seed safe (Here some examples).
At the moment the only supported browsers are Google Chrome and Brave.
